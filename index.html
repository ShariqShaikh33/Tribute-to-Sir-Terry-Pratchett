Terence David John Pratchett

1948 - 2015

Terry Pratchett was the acclaimed creator of the Global bestselling Discworld series, the first of which, The Colour of Magic, was published in 1983. In all, he was the author of over fifty bestselling books. His novels have been widely adapted for stage and screen, and he was the winner of multiple prizes, including the Carnegie Medal, as well as being awarded a knighthood for services to literature.

The Discworld

Illustrated By josh kirby

This is an absurd fantasy world created by Terry Pratchett, to create satirical parellels with our real world. The series consists a total of 41 books. It starts from The Colour OF Magic to The Sheperd's Crown in the order of publication, But the publication order is just a suggestion that you dont have to take seriously. Every single book in discworld is written to be a stand alone read. But the whole series is not just 41 stand alone titles, The whole Discworld can be divided into seperate story arcs which follow certain cast of characters through multiple books.
These Story-arcs include :
1) Ankh-Morpork City Watch.
2) Death.
3) Witches.
Many) Rincewind,
Many-1) Tiffany Aching
Many-2) Industrial Revolution.
Many-3) Ancient Civilizations.
There are also few truly One-shot books that dont fit in any of the above Arcs.
The most appealing part of Discworld for me is that its Characters are not written to fit the World but the World is molded to fit the Characters.
